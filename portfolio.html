European Brand — SS25

40 Styles in 12 Weeks

A European high-street brand needed a full flats range from concept to delivery in under 3 months. We ran parallel sample development across categories, delivered 40 production-ready styles. 34 went into final buy.

  • 40 styles sampled
  • 34 approved for production
  • 12 weeks concept to shelf
REAL PHOTO: Close-up of hand-embroidered mule — thread detail and beading work
Indian Premium Label — AW25

From Embroidery House to Footwear

A brand known for apparel embroidery wanted to extend their signature work onto footwear. We adapted their textile techniques for shoe uppers, developed new lasting methods for embellished materials. Three seasons in, it's their bestselling category.

  • 3 seasons ongoing
  • #1 selling category
  • 8 embroidery techniques adapted
REAL PHOTO: Resort sandals in metallic and natural tones — lifestyle shot on marble surface
Dubai Brand — Resort 25

Middle East Resort Collection

A Dubai-based fashion brand needed resort-wear sandals with Middle Eastern design influences — metallic leathers, geometric laser-cut patterns, and ornamental hardware. We developed 25 styles across sandals, mules, and embellished flats. The collection shipped to retail in 3 markets.

  • 25 styles delivered
  • 3 categories covered
  • 3 markets launched
