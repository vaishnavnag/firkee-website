03 Factory Tour

Inside the Factory

01

Material Store

Leathers, textiles, soles, hardware — all inspected on arrival for colour consistency, grain quality, and defects. Climate-controlled storage for sensitive materials. Organized by material type and supplier for fast retrieval.

02

Cutting

Uppers and components cut from optimised patterns using hydraulic clicking presses and skilled hand-cutters. We track material yield closely — less waste, lower cost. Pattern nesting software ensures maximum use of each hide.

03

Stitching

Components assembled into the upper on industrial flat-bed and post-bed machines. Edge folding, binding, skiving, and decorative stitching happen here. Each operator specializes in specific stitch types for consistency across the run.

04

Lasting

Uppers pulled over lasts and shaped using heat activation and manual tension. This is where fit and silhouette get locked in. We maintain our own last library across all size ranges and can develop custom lasts for new profiles.

05

Finishing

Sole attachment using heat-activated adhesives and hydraulic presses. Trimming, edge painting, polishing, and final detailing by hand. Every surface checked before it moves to QC — stitch tension, adhesion, colour match, symmetry.

06

Quality Control

Multi-point inspection to AQL 2.5 standard: fit, finish, colour match, structural integrity, sole adhesion, and packaging accuracy. Every pair checked against your approved sample. Defective pairs are pulled before packing.

07

Packing

Each pair stuffed, tissue-wrapped, and boxed to your spec. Inner and outer carton labelling, barcode scanning, and weight verification. Custom branded packaging or private label. Palletized and ready for container loading.
